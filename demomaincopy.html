InnovITy Club

InnovITy is a club organized by Department of Information Technology. Every semester two events will be conducted one technical and non-techical. A cash prize of Rs 2000 is given to the prize winners in every semester. This club is highly to motivate the students in participating the events and improve their technical skills . Various events like Code Debugging, Technical Quiz, Googling, Poster Design , Adzap, Techno Talk, Talk show, Group Discussion etc.
